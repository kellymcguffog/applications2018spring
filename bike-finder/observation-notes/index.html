Kelly and James's "Buying A Bike" Notes

Steps Taken

  • Google bike
  • Google shopping
  • Browse through options
  • Filter by: Gender (Women), Color, Price ($150-350)
  • Browse through the new options
  • Click on bike for info: Schwinn Wayfarer 700C Womens Hybrid Retro, City Bike, Green, Light Mint
  • Shopping link: JCPenny
  • Confirm Item Descripton, Shipping Method, and Quantity
  • Add Item to Cart
  • Continue to Checkout

Step Description

1. Not knowing where to find an online bike shop, the shopper would use search engine to help with the shopping process.
2. Browse through the initial options, i.e. before falling back to filters.
3. Use filters to eliminate less-preferred/irrelevant options.
4. Browse through the filtered options.
5. Find the preferred item. Check on item description/peripherals.
6. Repeat step 5 if necessary. Compare all possible options for best solution.
7. Add item to cart.
8. Head over to cart.
9. Follow the website checkout process.
